FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

01. Where can I buy your products? 

You buy the sensors directly from us. Contact us for prices

or if you want help finding the right product for your application.


02. Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship internationally to most parts of the world.

Please observe that import taxes may occur and if so they are to be paid bý the customer.


03.  Who should I contact if I have of any queries?

You will find our Contact details above.


04.  How can I cancel or change my order?

Orders can in many times be cancelled or changed if we get notice in reasonable time before shipping.

Send us an e-mail to check.


05.  How can I return a product?

It is important to us that you are satisfied with your purchase.

Please contact us if you should have any problems with the product.

In case of manufacturing errors, we will naturally replace the faulty product with a new one without charge.


06.  How long will it take to get my package?

We use FedEx priority for shipping outside Sweden. You willl receive a tracking number when the order is sent.

If items are in stock, your order will normally be sent from us wittin 24 hours. Exceptions may occur.

Fedex priority shippings will arrive within 1-2 work days to most parts of the world.


07.  How much is shipping?

Shipping by FedEx Priority within Western Europe or to North America is 28 EUR for standard sensors.

For larger/heavier items than standard sensors, the shipping cost may be higher.

Check with us for prices to other parts of the world.

We can also ship on your own shipping account after agreement.


08.  How is payment done?

We normally send you an invoice for payment directly to our bank account.

If you want to pay by credit card, we will send you a payment link through Stripe. An extra card payment fee of 4,65 % will be added to the total amount.
